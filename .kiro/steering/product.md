# Product Overview

ThinkCanvas is a desktop application that combines an infinite canvas with AI-powered thinking assistance. It enables users to create visual knowledge networks by placing nodes on an infinite canvas, connecting ideas, and engaging with AI to explore thoughts in a non-linear way.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Visual Thinking Space**: Unlimited canvas for organizing ideas spatially
- **AI Thinking Partner**: Conversational AI that helps explore, question, and deepen understanding
- **Knowledge Persistence**: All nodes, connections, and conversations are automatically saved locally
- **Non-linear Exploration**: Build thought networks by connecting related concepts

## Key Features

- Infinite canvas with pan, zoom, and minimap navigation
- Node creation and editing with customizable colors and sizes
- Visual connections between nodes to show relationships
- AI chat panel that creates new connected nodes from responses
- Auto-save to IndexedDB for local persistence
- Import text to quickly create multiple nodes
- Keyboard shortcuts for efficient workflow
- Support for multiple AI providers (OpenAI, DeepSeek, Ollama)

## Target Use Cases

- Personal knowledge management and note-taking
- Brainstorming and idea exploration
- Research and concept mapping
- Learning and study organization
- Creative writing and planning

## Product Philosophy

ThinkCanvas treats thinking as a spatial, iterative process. Unlike linear note-taking tools, it encourages users to place ideas where they make sense visually, connect related concepts, and use AI as a collaborative thinking partner rather than just a question-answering tool.
